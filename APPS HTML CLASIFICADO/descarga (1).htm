About IMGinn.io

IMGinn.io is a website that provides an online profile search engine: Imginn, designed to be simple and easy to use, allowing users to view and download all the content that has been posted on the social network Instagram. To do that, we have allocated separate search engines for each content category including: profiles, Photos, Videos, Reels and Stories Highlights! Tools Imginn work on public and private accounts, if you want full sized and high quality photos or videos then IMGinn.io is the perfect place for you!

Why do you need to use Imginn?

Finding the profile of a friend, stranger, or celebrity on Instagram can be challenging, but using Imginn makes it easy. If the account is private, you will only get basic information like username and thumbnail; however, if you want to know more information or download the full content of that person or your own then nothing can stop you at Imginn with all the following great features!

  1. Easily search anyone's profile on Instagram and view it in full: profile picture, post count, followers,...
  2. You can even fully zoom and then download your profile picture in full size.
  3. Not only your profile picture, but all the images in your Instagram collection are easily downloaded in the best quality.
  4. Watch and download full HD videos or Reels quickly.
  5. Private stories or featured stories you can enjoy them and also download.

All the tools that we provide to our users are always safe, free and especially anonymous!

Instructions for using the tools of IMGinn.io

As the introduction we mentioned above, the tools are designed to be extremely simple and easy to use, just follow these 3 steps:

  • Step 1: Select the tool you want to use.
